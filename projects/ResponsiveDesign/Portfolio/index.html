The ever important background story

Spring 2019 I started my journey through the freeCodeCamp curriculum. Well, technically not 100% true, since I had a failed start a few years earlier, but this is my main push. This page will be a portfolio showcasing my poor attempts at building the projects needed to complete this very ambitious course.

This page will be divided into sections mirroring the curriculum. This means you will get six main sections, and maybe some others if I come up with cool projects on the side. Don't hold your breath for those, as I have no real ideas for anything right now. I will rebuild my person website sometime and also create a page for a friend of mine, but when that happens depends on many variables, some of which I have little control.

So far there are only Responsive Web Design projects on this page, but the rest of the curriculum will be added as I get to the projects.

If you stumble upon my freeCodeCamp profile page, you might find some links to projects I did when I prematurely started this journey a few years back. Please don't look at them. They are ugly, probably not working and will only make you point and laugh at me... :D

But enough about me and without further ado, let's get the portfolio started!
